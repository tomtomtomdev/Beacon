- **2026-07-08** — **Slice 5 shipped (cross-source dedup); decisions during the build:** (1) **Match key = exact `(company_id, normalized_title, country)` AND simhash(description) within Hamming ≤ 8** (SPEC §5 key 2). The exact title+company+country tuple is the *primary* false-merge guard ("Senior iOS" vs "Senior Android" never collide because titles differ); the simhash only has to reject the rare generic-title collision ("Software Engineer" twice at one company for two unrelated roles). `normalize_title` is deliberately conservative — lowercase + whitespace-collapse only; punctuation differences keep titles distinct (under-merge > over-merge). (2) **simhash hand-rolled in `domain/dedup.py`** (pure, no dep — PLAN allowed a lib but stdlib-first + it's central to correctness): 64-bit Charikar simhash over lowercased alphanumeric tokens weighted by frequency, hashed with **blake2b (deterministic across processes)** not the salted builtin `hash()`. (3) **Threshold 8 chosen on evidence, not to pass a test:** measured on a realistic ~77-token JD — whitespace/punct-only diff = 0 bits, short footer = 1, added sentence = 1, a 15-word footer = 7; a different eng role = 22, a marketing role = 14. 8 sits in the wide 7↔14 gap, biased toward merge (showing the same job twice is the worse sin). **simhash is noisy on very short text** (a 5-word footer on a 28-token doc flips 11 bits) — tests therefore use realistic-length descriptions; the domain boundary test (`test_dedup.py`) documents the near/far separation. (4) **Canonical = lowest id in a cluster** (earliest-inserted, stable/deterministic); clustering is transitive union-find within each group. Canonical row keeps `canonical_id = NULL`; duplicates point at it. `assign_canonicals(rows) -> {id: canonical_id}` is **pure** (fast unit tests, no DB); the `dedupe_jobs` use case just applies the mapping via the repo. **Idempotent** — re-running yields the same links. (5) **Dedup runs as a post-upsert pass over the whole DB** (PLAN task wording), once after `ingest_all` — a Greenhouse job can't be compared to a Lever job until both are persisted.
